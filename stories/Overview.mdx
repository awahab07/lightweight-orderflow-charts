# Lightweight Order Flow Charts

Reusable order-flow charts and studies for `lightweight-charts`, with both vanilla helpers and React
bindings.

## What you can inspect here

- **Full Charts** show realistic chart compositions built from the publishable package surface.
- **Study Components** isolate primitives and custom-series integrations such as delta summary,
  session profiles, and VWAP.
- **Theming and Styling** exposes the surface and candle palette controls with preset-aware args.

## Data note

The fixture data used in these stories is locally crafted for demonstration and validation. It is
not intended to represent complete or live market conditions.
